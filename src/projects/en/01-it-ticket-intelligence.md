End-to-end ML pipeline for IT ticket intelligence at a global manufacturing enterprise: raw data ingestion from ServiceNow APIs, semantic embeddings, UMAP dimensionality reduction, auto-tuned HDBSCAN clustering, and a multi-stage LLM orchestration layer (Azure AI Foundry) for text normalisation, topic labelling and Knowledge Base generation. Interactive dashboards on thousands of incidents and service requests.
